What Is Mediation?

Mediation is a form of alternative dispute resolution where a neutral third party, called a mediator, helps people involved in a disagreement come to a mutual agreement without going to court. It’s voluntary, confidential, and focuses on cooperation rather than conflict—resulting in quicker, cheaper, and more creative solutions that preserve relationships.
